When in the year the heat lands

Every qualifying day from every complete year since 1960, folded onto one calendar. Hot days spread across a broad summer, but ≥30 °C days and tropical nights compress into a narrow mid-July-to-August window — they need a run of settled heat, not just a warm spell. Hover any curve for a given day’s tally. The part-year 2026 is left out: it can supply days up to 15 Jul but none after, which would tug every distribution earlier.
